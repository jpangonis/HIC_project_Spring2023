ABOUT US

UNITED GAMING COMPANIES WORLDWIDE

Alliance Gaming is proud to be one of the best in it's field as it supports both its players and staff. Being able to have many teams makes us one of the best of our kind. Alliance Gaming has professional teams for Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Halo, League of Legends, Super Smash Bros. Melee, VALORANT, and more. Alliance is always looking to grow and have gained many more teams since it's founding.

Alliance Gaming is proud of it's accomplishments since its founding. In 2022 alone, there has been 50 tournaments attended with 21 first place winners. In addition, there were 20 2nd/3rd place winners on top of this.

Some of the biggest accomplishments are as follows:

  • 2022 League of Legends Champions
  • 2021 Korea Open Championship first place
  • Ludwig Ahgren Championship Series 3 first place

Some of our teams are:

  • CS:GO
  • Halo
  • League of Legends
  • League of Legends Challengers
