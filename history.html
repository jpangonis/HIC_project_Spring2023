History

foundation

Founded in 2015, Alliance Gaming is one of the most well known esports organizations in the world. Being one of the first large scale esports organization has helped grow the company to be the best of it's kind. The founders, Ben and Will Alli, created this company after their parents had a great passion for esports. Growing up with this culture helped grow their love for esports and inspired them to create an organization where there is a community for people who share the passion for esports.

resreach

Alliance Gaming has studied the best way to train other esport athletes to become the best player that they can be. At the start of the company, there was a lot of research done involving the way esport athletes excell in their game. The conclusion was that an environment where other players can challenge each other best prepares them for competitions. Alliance has also done research in game design to try and develop a game of it's own. Stay tuned!
