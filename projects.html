the alliance academy



The Alliance Academy is our way of giving back to the academy. A promising group of individuals are selected based on their willingness to grow and ability to improve, and then put through rigorous trianing, with a handful of resources, completely fre of cost in order to prepare them to become ready to take on profession teams and esports atheletes in the real world. We basically provide free training to those who wsih to pursue a serious career in E-Sports. So far, we've helped hundreds as a part of this program.
